Food Storage Tips

Learn how to store your food properly to extend its shelf life and reduce waste.

Fruits and Vegetables

Store fruits and vegetables separately. Some fruits emit ethylene gas, which can cause vegetables to spoil faster.

Meat and Dairy

Keep meat and dairy products in the coldest part of your refrigerator to maintain freshness.

Meal Planning

Plan your meals for the week to avoid buying unnecessary items and wasting food.

Creating a Meal Plan

List out meals for each day and create a shopping list based on the ingredients needed.

Composting

Learn how to compost your food scraps to reduce waste and create nutrient-rich soil.

What to Compost

Fruit and vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, and eggshells are great for composting.
